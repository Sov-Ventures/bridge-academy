Your Bridge Into Crypto

Bridge Academy is a fully distributed, invite-only, career accelerator program built for top developers interested in getting into crypto.

Gain hands-on experience, reputation & earn coins for contributing to open-source projects.

Apply To Join The Next Batch

Takes just 2 minutes to apply



How it works

1) Apply to join the program

Fill out our simple application form to apply to join our 3-month long program. We run batches every three months and encourage people to re-apply.

2) Earn while you learn

Participate in challenges to gain hands-on experience and build up your reputation, earning crypto bonuses for completing tasks successfully.

3) Land your dream job

Once the program ends, you'll have the opportunity to interview at projects hiring for full-time as well as part-time contractor roles.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the program last?

The part-time program lasts 10 weeks and is broken into 3 phases. The full-time program is 5 weeks. New batches open up every 3 months.

What happens when I find a job?

Once you find a job through our network, you will receive a signing bonus equal to 5% of your annual salary.

Is there any cost to participate?

While there is no application fee, we do ask for $500 deposit which will be refunded after completing Phase 3 of the program.

How do I know if i'm qualified?

Most developers who apply have at least 2-3 years of development experience. Most don't have any prior crypto knowledge.

How does the referral program work?

Refer a qualified particpant and get a bonus of $500 for each person that successfully applies & is accepted.

Can I particpate while employed?

Full-time jobs are discouraged since it's too difficult to dedicate enough time. Part-time jobs are permitted for the part-time program.
